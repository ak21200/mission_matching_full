Stage 1 will consist of five rounds. Each round will last for 90 seconds.

In each round, you will see a screen with 48 sliders. Each slider will start out at a random position.

Your objective is to move as many sliders to the centre of the slider bar as you can.

The centre of the slider bar is marked by a red dot. You will know you have correctly positioned the slider at the centre of the bar when the slider turns blue.

The screen will contain a timer showing you how much time remains in the round, and a progress bar showing how many sliders you have completed.

Each round will begin immediately after the end of the previous round.

If this stage is chosen to determine your earnings for the experiment, you will receive £1.50 per round, for a total of £7.50.
